The Best Movies According to Nick

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Tommy Boy

A favorite due to Chris Farley and his stupid humor. I used to be able to quote this movie in its entirety.

Boondock Saints

An action movie with a lot of blood and shooting. I enjoy the humor that is sprinkled in.

Clerks

A movie that proved you do not need a lot of money to make a hit. I also enjoyed Clerks 2 and 3.
